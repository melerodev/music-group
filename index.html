Our History

Formed in 1983, Bon Jovi became one of the biggest rock bands in the world, known for anthems like "Livin' on a Prayer" and "It's My Life." With over 130 million records sold, they continue to shape the music industry.

Band Members

  • Jon Bon Jovi - Lead Vocals, Guitar
  • David Bryan - Keyboards, Backing Vocals
  • Tico Torres - Drums
  • Phil X - Lead Guitar
  • Hugh McDonald - Bass
